Name: Buzz's Mixer
Format: 4x32J
Source: Dance a While, p342
Notes: Form two circles, men inside, women outside, men face the women
1-4	Advance retire and cross, turning lady under right arm.
5-8	Repeat bars 1-4 back to own side.
9-12	Swing by taking waltz hold with partner and twirling around.
13-16	Promenade
17-20	Ladies cross in front of men as men turn around
21-24	Men go clockwise along the inside ring as women go anticlockwise on the outside
25-28	Promenade with new partner
29-32	Swing with new partner
